Important Notes

  • Use EVERY book and lamp on Herblore until you reach level 77. After 77 Herblore , put them on Prayer (if Hardcore Ironman ), Agility , or RC (whichever you hate most)
  • Enable Authenticator on your account
  • Make sure to start with a FEMALE character (we use this later for Recruitment Drive (it saves 3K GP))
  • Before leaving Tutorial Island, make sure you stop at the NODE to get the group together and make a Group Ironman

Lumbridge 1 - Starting up and Gathering

  • Upon arriving in Lumbridge, sell your bronze dagger, sword, axe, wooden shield, and shortbow to the general store
  • Buy a spade and then start X Marks the Spot
  • Drop your runes and ask for more from the magic tutor, pickup your runes after you receive the free ones
  • Pickup every item spawn in the castle, including the cellar, don't forget your cabbage!
  • Fill a jug with water in the kitchen or the cellar
  • Head to the 2nd floor and talk to King Roald to start Rune Mysteries , then head to the 3rd floor and bank everything
  • Light the 4 logs on the ground until 15 Firemaking
  • After getting level 15, Fletch 1000 arrow shafts using the same logs
  • Bank at least 4 ashes and 7 logs for later
  • Withdraw GP, clue from X Marks the Spot , air talisman, spade, mind + air runes, bread and shrimp from the bank
  • Level your thieving to level 5 on men
  • If Hardcore Ironman , eat bread/shrimp, hop worlds for cabbages from basement for food.
  • Dig up the clue north of Bob's Brilliant Axes
  • Buy a steel axe (make sure the world is fully stocked, the axe should only cost 200gp)
  • Start The Restless Ghost by talking to Father Aereck
  • Dig up the clue behind Lumbridge castle
  • Use wind strike to kill a giant rat for it's meat.
  • Hug the fence and run west, make sure to keep some run energy incase you need to evade anything that might attack you
  • Pick up 5 swamp tar around the swamp cave entrance
  • Talk to Father Urhney to get the Ghostspeak amulet and equip it

Draynor, Port Sarim, Falador, and Rimmington 1

  • Take the air talisman to the Wizard's Tower
  • Dig up the next clue for X Marks the Spot
  • Pick up 2 cheese at Aggie's house in Draynor Village
  • Buy 5 wines from Fortunato for emergency food if necessary
  • Dig up the casket at the Draynor pig pen
  • Finish X Marks the Spot by talking to Veos, DO NOT use the XP lamp just yet
  • Walk over to Rimmington, pick up 1 snape grass near the crafting guild by the hobgoblins (don't waste run here so you can run in and out)
  • Walk to Falador, start The Knight's Sword by talking to the Squire
  • Minigame teleport to Clan Wars (Ferox Enclave)

Yanille, Arganoog, and Port Khazard

  • Pick up 2 iron bars just North West of Ferox Enclave
  • Withdraw GP, steel axe, tinderbox, jug of water, 1 log, and jug of wine from the bank
  • Recharge your run energy by using the pool of refreshment, then use the green portal to teleport to Castle Wars
  • Walk East over to Yanille and buy a pie dish at the Yanille Cooking Shop
  • Walk North East to Port Khazard and buy 1 swamp paste from the general store
  • Complete Monk's Friend
  • Get 35 Woodcutting at oak trees and Firemake the logs as you go
  • Head to Arganoog and steal from the baker's stall until 20 Thieving (bank 15 bread, ALL cakes, drop chocolates)
  • Steal silk until 25 Thieving
  • Complete Sheep Herder
  • Withdraw GP, swamp paste, and a small fishing net
  • Complete Sea Slug , fish shrimp while doing quest for the diary (keep the oyster pearls, they're over 500gp in the general store)
  • Buy as many sardines as you can fit in your inventory from the fish shop in Witchaven
  • Sell the silk for 60gp/ea, keep 10 in the bank for quests, you should have 10k GP now
  • Buy a Player-Owned House, then use the XP lamp from X Marks the Spot on Construction
  • Buy 2 ropes, 5 vials, 30 balls of wool, and 7 papyrus from the Arganoog General Store
  • Withdraw GP, rope, spade, and a few cakes
  • Dig up the Clue Hunter gloves and boots north of Arganoog

Barbarians, Waterfalls, and Gnomes

  • Buy a Greenman's ale from Rasolo
  • Start Dwarf Cannon up until you need to go to Ice Mountain
  • Pick up 130 planks north of the Barbarian Outpost Agility Course
  • Complete the Barbarian Assault tutorial to unlock the minigame teleport (you can skip, just make sure you can teleport there before you leave)
  • Start Alfred Grimhand's Barcrawl
  • Start Waterfall Quest until you need to go to the gnome maze (remember to read the book)
  • Walk over to the Gnome Stronghold
  • Buy a drink from Blurberry as part of Alfred Grimhand's Barcrawl
  • Buy the following items from Heckel Funch (East of Blurberry)
    1 Pineapple
    1 Orange
    2 Lemons
    1 Cocktail Shaker
    1 Cocktail Glass
    1 Dwellberries
    1 Bucket of Milk
    1 Chocolate Dust
  • Buy 1000 bronze arrowheads (100/world for 1gp/ea) at the wepon shop on the 3rd floor South of the ladder
  • Make the hangover cure (bucket of milk, chocolate dust, and snape grass) for Plague City
  • Walk to Arganoog

Return to Arganoog, heading to Varrock, 50 Woodcutting/Firemaking

  • Complete Plague City
  • Home teleport to Lumbridge and run North to Varrock East bank
  • Equip your Ghostspeak amulet (if you banked it at some point)
  • Complete Daddy's Home
  • Sell the 8 bolts of cloth from the quest reward back to the sawmill
  • Buy 500 bronze nails at the sawmill
  • Minigame teleport to Clan Wars, recharge energy, and use the portal to Castle Wars
  • Withdraw your steel axe and a tinderbox
  • Run South-West of Castle Wars
  • Woodcut and Firemake teak logs until you have 50 Firemaking
  • Home teleport back to Lumbridge
  • Talk to the ghost for The Restless Ghost
  • Run to Draynor bank

Return to Draynor, Starting Construction, Visiting Great Kourend

  • Withdraw the following from the bank
    GP
    Bronze Nails
    Hammer
    Saw
    120 Noted Planks
    1k Arrows Shafts
    1k Bronze Arrowheads
    1 Teleport to House Tablet
  • Buy a Chronicle and 2 teleport cards from Diango
  • Use your Teleport to House Tablet and train Construction with your planks, make bookcases until out of planks using Phials to un-note planks
  • Walk South to Port Sarim
  • Buy 1000 feathers from the fishing store (you should have just enough GP, if not it's not a big deal)
  • Take the boat to Great Kourend
  • Make the long trek to Wintertodt, fletch 1k bronze arrows while walking
  • Note: If you see wolves, you went the wrong way, you should not be attacked.

The First Grind

  • Do Wintertodt until you have at least 200k GP, you also need 22 Fletching
  • Note: You shouldn't be dying with only 10HP, but attention is still necessary, it's a long walk back. Make sure you wear the Clue Hunter Boots and Gloves.
  • OPTIONAL: Stay until you have 12 Magic Logs for Desert Treasure
  • OPTIONAL: Get 99 Firemaking, don't open the crates in your bank once you have 200k GP

Lumbridge 2

  • Withdraw the following from the bank
    GP
    A few cakes
    Chronicle
    ALL of your air and mind runes
    Teleport to House Tablet
    Teleport to Lumbridge Tablet
  • Run to the Mage Tutor, drop your runes and grab more (don't forget to pickup your runes)
  • Buy 3 Buckets and 1 Bucket Pack from the General Store
  • Walk North to Fred's Farm
  • Kill a chicken with wind strike for it's meat
  • Pickup the egg to the north of Fred
  • Kill 1 cow calf for meat
  • Fill 3 buckets with milk at the dairy cow north of the windmill
  • Walk up to Barbarian Village (you can walk up East of the Draynor Manor)
  • Grab the ammo mould and the notes for Dwarf Cannon
  • Use the Chronicle teleport, walk North to Gertrude's house

Varrock 1

  • Start Gertrude's Cat , talk to the kids at the Varrock square
  • Talk to Romeo to start Romeo & Juliet
  • Start Demon Slayer , grab the 2 keys located in Varrock
  • Talk to Reldo for The Knight's Sword
  • Start Shield of Arrav on any visit to Varrock
  • Buy a fire staff
  • Buy a newspaper from Benny
  • Buy gloves and a pink skirt from the clothes shop
  • Buy a rotten tomato from the crate near Toby
  • Get a drink from the bar south of the Varrock fountain for Alfred Grimhand's Barcrawl
  • Buy 4 beers from the same bar
  • Do the Natural History Quiz for 9 Hunter and Slayer
  • Steal tea from the tea stall
  • Give the package from the Rune Mysteries quest to Aubury in the rune shop South of Varrock East Bank, speak to him again for the notes
  • Buy 100 fire runes, 300 earth and water runes, 4000 mind runes, and 8000 air runes
  • Withdraw the following from the bank
    GP
    Barcrawl card
    Bucket of Milk
    Seasoned sardine
    Axe
  • Walk to the pub North-East of Varrock
  • Get a drink for Alfred Grimhand's Barcrawl
  • Cut a dying tree at the sawmill and make 1 regular plank for diary tasks
  • Continue Gertrude's Cat at the lumberyard
  • Walk over to the Varrock East bank
  • Withdraw a pickaxe and the Chronicle from your bank
  • Fill 1 inventory of buckets with water and bank them
  • Pick Cadava berries and 16 Red berries
  • Mine 4 copper ore and 1 iron ore
  • Finish Gertrude's Cat , raise the cat with the sardines you bought
  • Talk to Juliet
  • Walk to the Varrock West bank
  • Withdraw from bank
    GP
    Runes
    Barcrawl card
    Cakes
    Staff of Fire (Equip)
  • Walk to Edgeville

Draynor, Port Sarim, Falador, and Rimmington 2

  • Start Enter the Abyss by talking to the Zamorak Mage in the Wildnerness
  • Walk towards Falador
  • Buy a bronze med helm from Barbarian Village
  • Pick up 1 cooked meat from the table and burn it on the fire in the bar
  • Safespot barbarians in the bar for 13 magic (or skip this and get 13 Magic during Witch's House )
  • Use the trees to safespot a bear for it's meat South of the mind altar
  • Head to Falador, get a drink from the Falador bar for Alfred Grimhand's Barcrawl
  • Buy 5 Wizard Mind Bombs from the bar
  • Buy 8 woad leaves, offer 20gp to get 2 leaves
  • Head to the bank
  • Withdraw the following items:
    GP
    Cheese
    Runes
    All 4 meats for Druidic Ritual
    18 Cakes
    Gloves (Equip)
  • Complete Witch's House (Tank first 2 phases on North side of the shed, run to the safespot when you need to eat.)
  • Complete Druidic Ritual
  • Buy 1 PACK of eyes of newt and a pestle and mortar in Taverly
  • Withdraw the following items:
    GP
    Pickaxe
    Burnt meat
    Eye of newt
    2 Iron bars
    Bucket of water
    Pie dish
    10 cakes
    2 Teleport to House tablets
    1 Falador Teleport Tablet
  • Use your house tab to get to Rimmington
  • Complete Witch's Potion, the onion is just north of the town
  • Run to Port Sarim and make a redberry pie (flour and redberries are avaialble at the Port Sarim food shop)
  • Get a security book from the Security Guard upstairs in the Port Sarim Jail for a diary task
  • Give the redberry pie to Thurgo, and ask about The Knight's Sword
  • Go down into the dungeon and mine 2 blurite ore, 1 is for a Falador diary task
  • Home teleport and run to Al-Kharid

Al-Kharid and Port Khazard

  • Buy 50 thread, 3 needles, and all of the moulds from the crafting shop
  • Start Prince Ali Rescue and talk to Osman
  • Fish anchovies (using a small fishing net) south of the Al-Kharid bank for a diary task
  • Buy 1 inventory of bronze bars for questing from the shop at Shantay Pass
  • Complete The Tourist Trap, during the quest it is recommended to use this safespot. Put BOTH XP rewards on Agility
  • Minigame teleport to the Fishing Trawler
  • Cook all the low level raw food you got from Wintertodt to get 15 Cooking at the fire
  • Buy 10 buckets of slime
  • Buy 10 flour from general store

The Second Grind: Grind Harder

  • Buy 600 buckets of sand and 600 soda ash, these will be used later for crafting

FAQ

The foundation of this guide was based on Zachary Kjellberg's Dark Souls 3 Cheat Sheet source code and formatting

Most of the guide itself is based on Oziris Ironman Guide v4 that was rewritten and formatted by NightleafTV at nightleaf.org.

I have feedback, how can I contribute?

You can either message me on Discord, or visit the GitHub repository and report Issues or create a fork and submit a Pull Request.

Is this a guide?

More or less, yes. It's more of an organized list with easily markable entries to keep track of progress.

Do I need to follow the playthrough in this exact order?

Absolutely not, deviate however you would like.

Can I use this for multiple characters?

Yup, use the profile selector and buttons in the options tab at the top of the page to setup multiple profiles.

How does the checklist status get saved?

The checklist is saved to your browser's local storage. Be careful when clearing your browser's cache as it will also destroy your saved progress.
